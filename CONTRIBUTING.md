# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving this methodology.

## Suggest an idea

Open a **Discussion** — great for proposing new phases, challenging existing ones, or sharing how you've adapted the methodology.

## Suggest a specific change

1. Fork the repo
2. Edit `README.md` (or add files)
3. Open a Pull Request with a clear description of what you changed and why

All PRs are reviewed by the maintainer before merging.

## Guidelines

- Keep suggestions practical and grounded in real agentic engineering experience
- One change per PR — easier to review and discuss
- Link to examples or projects where possible
